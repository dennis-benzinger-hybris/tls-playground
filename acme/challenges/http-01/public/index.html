Welcome Client!

This plain HTTP web-server is only here to complete ACME http-01 challenges.

As mandated by ACME, challenges will be exposed unter /.well-known/acme-challenge/.
Note that this directory will be empty, unless you are currently in the process of ordering an certificate through ACME.

Once challenges have been completed and certificates have been installed, check out the HTTPS Demo Servers instead!
Simply go to your web-browser's address bar, replace the initial http:// with https:// and press enter or click the Go button.
